## Limited Reproducibility Experiments

Each study published their artifacts. 
We uploaded additional files that helped us to set up the repositories. 

We reproduce the findings using a server with an AMD
EPYC 7713 (64-cores, 128-threads), 256GB RAM, two
RTX A6000 with 96GB VRAM in total, 1.8TB SSD and
Ubuntu 24 or with a MacBook Pro (14-inch, 2021) with
a 10-core CPU, a 16-core GPU, 1TB of SSD storage, and
32GB of RAM.

Original repositories from all studies that were reproduced:

- [AdFlush](https://zenodo.org/records/10682483)
- [DUUMVIRI](https://github.com/dlgroupuoft/Duumviri-NDSS25/tree/main)
- [PURL](https://github.com/shaoormunir/purl)
- [WTAGraph](https://github.com/jun521ju/IEEE_SP_2022_WtaGraph)
- [T.EX Graph](https://github.com/t-ex-tools/t.ex-graph-2.0-classifier)
- [Response](https://github.com/wolfrieder/http-response-classifier.git)
- [Byte-Learn](https://github.com/byte-learn/byte-learn)
